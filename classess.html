CLASSES

Carnatic Music is the great classical music art form of South India. With its ancient origins, it is the oldest musical form that is still in practice. Today, Carnatic music is spreading all around the world and through the internet is getting an even wider audience. Carnatic Corner lets you explore this fascinating art to the fullest with links to audio, information about leading practitioners and organisations as well as an extensive library of knowledge.
